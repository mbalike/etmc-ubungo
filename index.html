The End-Time Messenger

William Marrion Branham

We believe that God, in fulfilment of Malachi 4:5–6 and Revelation 10:7, sent a prophet in our day — William Marrion Branham — whose vindicated ministry turned the hearts of this generation back to the apostolic fathers and opened the hidden mysteries of the Word of God.

Born in 1909 in Kentucky, Bro. Branham’s ministry was accompanied by extraordinary supernatural signs: healing the sick, discerning the thoughts of hearts, and raising the dead — all pointing to the Pillar of Fire that commissioned him. His message is not a denomination; it is the Word restored.
