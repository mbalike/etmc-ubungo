About Us

Ubungo End Time Message Church

Who We Are

Ubungo End Time Message Church is a fellowship of born-again believers who have received the end-time Message brought by God’s servant and prophet William Marrion Branham, fulfilling Malachi 4:5-6 and Revelation 10:7.

We believe in the full restoration of the Word in this Laodicean age and seek to live a life led by the Holy Spirit, separated from the world, and fully surrendered to the Lord Jesus Christ.

Located in Ubungo, Dar es Salaam, we serve the local community with a global vision—to prepare a Bride ready for the rapture.

Our Mission

  • To preach the uncompromised Word as revealed through the prophet of this age.
  • To prepare souls for the coming of the Lord Jesus Christ.
  • To promote holy living, brotherly love, and spiritual growth.

What We Believe

One True God
We believe in one God, revealed in the Lord Jesus Christ.
Prophet’s Message
We believe William Branham was the promised Elijah of Malachi 4:5.
Bible Restoration
We believe the message of the hour is the restoration of the true apostolic faith.
Water Baptism
We practice baptism in the Name of the Lord Jesus Christ.
Holiness
We believe in a separated, holy life in word, dress, and conduct.
Rapture
We believe the rapture is at hand and that the Bride is being called out and prepared.

About Our Ministry

Ubungo End Time Message Church is committed to spreading the Word of God, touching lives through faith, and providing a path for spiritual growth. Join us as we worship, learn, and serve together in the love of Christ.
