Who We Are

Rooted in Scripture and led by the Spirit, we are a local body pursuing Christlike character, worship, discipleship, and service. We cherish apostolic doctrine and church order in love and humility.

“That in all things He might have the preeminence.” (Colossians 1:18)

Core Distinctives

Bible-Centered

The Infallible Word

Every chapter, verse, and line is the Absolute—our final authority for faith and life.

Apostolic

Full Gospel & Order

We teach apostolic blessing and maintain church order, doctrine, and discipline in love.

Five-Fold

Ephesians 4:11–16

We believe the five-fold ministry perfects and edifies the saints for the work of ministry.

Who is W. M. Branham? (Glimpse)

We recognize the end-time message promised in Malachi 4:5–6 and Revelation 10:7. We believe God sent the spirit of Elijah in our day—the end-time messenger to restore apostolic faith and reveal the mysteries of the Word, turning hearts of the children back to the fathers.
