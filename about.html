Core Distinctives

Bible-Centered

The Infallible Word

Every chapter, verse, and line is the Absolute — our final authority for faith, life, and doctrine.

Apostolic

Full Gospel & Order

We teach the full apostolic blessing and maintain sound church order, discipline, and love.

Five-Fold

Ephesians 4:11–16

We believe the five-fold ministry is God's ordained way to perfect and edify the body of Christ.

End-Time Message

Who is W. M. Branham?

We recognize the end-time message promised in Malachi 4:5–6 and Revelation 10:7. We believe God sent the spirit of Elijah in our day — an end-time messenger to restore apostolic faith and reveal the mysteries of the Word, turning the hearts of the children back to the fathers.
